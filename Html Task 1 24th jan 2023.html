LETS MAKE SOME FRIED RICE,HERE IS THE STEPS

Fried Rice is an Indo Chinese dish made by stir frying cooked rice along with a mix of vegetables, sauces in a wok. Fried Rice is an aromatic, mild yet flavourful dish and is fairly healthy with a lot of vegetables. Veg Fried Rice Recipe is explained here with step by step pictures and video.

INGREDENTS

  • Rice – You can use long grained basmati rice, regular basmati rice or even jasmine rice
  • Vegetables-Veggies is absolutely your choice you can add carrots, spring onion, mushrooms, beans, capsicum, cabbage etc
  • Sauces-Soya sauce is the main sauce used here. You can even add chilli sauce if you prefer.
  • Flavourings- Garlic and pepper adds more flavour to this dish.

Instructions

  1. Soak 1 cup basmati rice in water for 15 mins.
  2. Set aside undisturbed. Soaking helps in giving grain seperated and also soft fluffy rice.
  3. Rinse it well until starch water is washed off. Drain water completely.
  4. To a sauce pan add 4 cups water bring it a boil add required salt.
  5. Add drained rice to it.
  6. Give a quick mix and cook in low medium flame for 6-8 mins.
  7. Rice should be aldente soft but should have a bite. Rice should be soft but not mushy – only when pressed hard it should be mashed that is the right stage. Immediately switch off.
  8. Transfer to a colander drain water completely.
  9. Spread it over a plate to cool down. Set aside.
  10. Now to a wok – add 2 tbsp oil – add 1 tbsp garlic finely chopped and 1 tbsp spring onion white part.
  11. Saute for a minute.
  12. Add 1/2 cup carrot finely chopped, 1/2 cup capsicum, 3/4 cup cabbage shredded and 1/4 cup beans thinly sliced.
  13. Saute for 2 mins in high flame, keep stirring until the veggies start to shrink.
  14. Add salt to taste, 1 tsp vinegar,1 tsp soya sauce and 1 tsp pepper powder.
  15. Give a quick stir.
  16. Add cooked rice.
  17. Add 1/2 tsp crushed pepper, 1/4 cup spring onion green part.
  18. Tasty Veg Fried Rice is ready!
